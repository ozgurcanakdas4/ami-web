The Story

Our planet is at the edge of the global disaster, all living things are about to vanish. Lives and livelihoods are shattered, the world has become more dangerous. We play as the girl named Ami. She heads to a certain place that gives hope to survive. Yet, before she reaches it there are lots of challenges to take

What is AMI?

Ami is a puzzle-platformer with a deep story. Together with the main character you get to learn the drama of this world

  • Pleasant minimalist visual style to the atmosphere of the withering world

  • Focus on the character and object animation to show that the world is still alive

  • Silent story that is told through interaction with the environment, backgrounds and characters animation

  • Interesting puzzles to interact with the world and make gameplay more diverse

  • Sound design and music with love to every detail, makes you get the right mood
